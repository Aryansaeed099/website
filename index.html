Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide remotely. I'm experienced in remote collaboration using tools like Slack, Zoom, GitHub, and project management platforms. I'm available across multiple time zones to ensure smooth communication.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3-6 months. After discussing your requirements, I'll provide a detailed timeline with milestones for your specific project.

What services do you offer as a Full Stack Developer?

I offer end-to-end web development services including frontend development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React), backend development (Node.js, Python, Django), database design, API development, and deployment. I can build everything from simple websites to complex web applications.

What technologies do you recommend for my project?

The best technology stack depends on your project requirements, budget, and long-term goals. During our initial consultation, I'll analyze your needs and recommend the most suitable technologies to ensure scalability, performance, and maintainability.

How do you handle project maintenance after launch?

I offer various maintenance packages that include regular updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and feature enhancements. We can discuss a maintenance plan that fits your needs, whether it's on-demand support or a monthly retainer.
